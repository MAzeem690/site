Viral Solutions Case Study

A revolutionary application to offer an optimized testing and vaccination experience, ensuring broad accessibility and rapid results dissemination.

The Story of Viral Solutions 🤘

Viral Solutions, Southeast America’s beacon in COVID-19 management, made waves by spearheading Atlanta Public Schools’ first surveillance testing. Yet, they weren’t stopping there. In September 2020, with eyes set on the future, they embarked on a mission to redefine testing operations.

Their quest? To redefine data management, enrich patient data accuracy, expedite test results, and fortify HIPAA standards. In this ambitious pursuit, they teamed up with tech innovators, CarbonTeq.

The result? A groundbreaking tech solution that transformed Viral Solutions into a paragon of efficiency and agility.

Solution Provided by CarbonTeq

Understanding Honeycomb Credit’s pain points, CarbonTeq partnered with them to craft a platform customized to their unique requirements. This alliance brought forth transformative enhancements, encompassing:

  • Enhanced User Experience: A complete overhaul of the user interface, making investment processes more user-friendly.
  • Swift Investment Times: CarbonTeq’s optimizations drastically reduced the time taken for investment completions.
  • Workforce Automation: Manual tasks were automated, streamlining operations and improving efficiency.
  • Customization Capabilities: New features enabled better branding and user personalization, elevating the platform’s appeal.
  • Comprehensive Admin and Investor Portals: Consolidated data views provided clarity and insights for various stakeholders.

The Results 🤘

CarbonTeq’s transformative solution ushered in a new era of efficiency for Viral Solutions. Post-implementation, the milestones achieved include:

Automation Mastery
An impressive 65,000 test results were processed in just three months, with a staggering 2.4 million results to date.
Operational Efficiency
The automation eradicated the need for a large workforce for result communication, translating to significant cost and time savings.
Expedited Processes
Plate mapping processing time plummeted from an hour to a mere five minutes.

The enduring alliance between Viral Solutions and CarbonTeq stands as a testament to the might of sustained partnerships in navigating intricate challenges. Over a span of three years, their synergized efforts and continuous innovations have reshaped the landscape of COVID-19 testing, underscoring the power of collaboration in dynamic times.
